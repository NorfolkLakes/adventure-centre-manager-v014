Adventure Centre Manager v6.1.0

Programme Builder scheduling-engine correction:
- Campfire and Disco are restricted to Session 5 and scheduled together for all groups in a school.
- Auto Fill now builds one school-level activity plan and gives every group the same activity set.
- Super Break and Outdoor Challenge Canoe and Kayak each occupy valid two-session blocks.
- Bargain Special Canoe and Kayak use a consecutive S1-S2 or S3-S4 block.
- Standard activities cannot be duplicated.
- Backup activities are applied school-wide only when a requested activity cannot be scheduled.
- Auto Fill is atomic: an invalid programme is not applied.
- Programme Test checks blanks, duplicates, mismatched group plans, attendance windows, capacities and special activity rules.
