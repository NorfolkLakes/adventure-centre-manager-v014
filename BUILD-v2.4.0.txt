Adventure Centre Manager v2.4.0

Fixes:
- Programme days now remain chronological from the selected start date, including Friday to Monday.
- Programme screen and Excel export use the programme start date rather than Monday-first sorting.
- Canoe Lead + Canoe is a linked two-session block: Sessions 1-2 or 3-4 only.
- Kayak Lead + Kayak is a linked two-session block: Sessions 1-2 or 3-4 only.
- Selecting either half of a pair automatically fills and locks both sessions.
- Auto Build recognises requested lead/activity pairs and places them together.
- Publishing validates incomplete or incorrectly positioned paired water sessions.
- Archery capacity remains limited to one group per session.
