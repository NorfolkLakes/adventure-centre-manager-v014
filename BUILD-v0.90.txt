Adventure Centre Manager v0.90

- Added Admin > Activities & Equipment.
- Add, edit, enable, disable and safely remove activities.
- Record equipment quantity, activity colour, notes and maximum groups per session.
- Programme Builder now reads editable capacity limits instead of fixed limits.
- Disabled activities are excluded from new automatic programmes.
- Programme validation uses the saved capacity for each activity.
- Canoeing and kayaking are created as paired two-group rotations in consecutive sessions, with groups swapping activities.
- Existing professional Excel programme export retained.
- Existing professional portrait School Programme printing retained.
