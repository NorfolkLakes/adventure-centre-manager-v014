Adventure Centre Manager v2.3.0

Fixes:
- Daily Staffing selections are no longer reset by automatically adding all staff back to existing days.
- Dashboard live staff count now uses the real current session and staff_days_off status.
- Dashboard no longer substitutes another selected programme day when today is outside the loaded programme.
- Archery is hard-limited by SOP to one group per session, including old saved activity settings.
- Auto Build continues to plan the full school stay and validates capacity before publishing.
- Friday-to-Monday builder dates remain chronological.
