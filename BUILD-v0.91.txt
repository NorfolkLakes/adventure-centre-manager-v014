Adventure Centre Manager v0.91

Changes:
- Canoe and Kayak are generated as an adjacent two-session paired rotation.
- The first full day after arrival is preferred; arrival and departure days are fallback options.
- Existing manually arranged Canoe/Kayak rotations are recognised as complete.
- Update Programme no longer inserts an extra Canoe or Kayak session after a completed manual rotation.
- Manual activity selection checks capacity across all schools in the same day/session.
- Capacity warning names the affected schools and allows Cancel or explicit override.
- Drag-and-drop programme edits use the same capacity check.
