Adventure Centre Manager v0.70

Fixes the Days Off & Sickness calendar connection to Daily Staffing.

- Programme day labels such as FRI are now resolved to the calendar ISO date.
- Cross-month programme titles such as 29th June - 5th July are supported.
- Sick, OFF, HOL, AM OFF and PM OFF records now drive summary totals, manual selection and Auto Fill.
- Daily Staffing counters now read the same staff_days_off records as the calendar.
