Adventure Centre Manager v2.0 – SOP Engine Foundation

Added:
- Activity Library replaces Activities & Equipment.
- Per-activity staffing rules, minimum staffing and priorities.
- Required and lead qualification fields.
- Maximum participant/group value.
- Shareable-session and safety-boat controls.
- Weather/environment restrictions.
- Setup and pack-away checklists.
- Emergency procedure, SOP reference and Risk Assessment reference.
- Existing saved activities migrate automatically with safe defaults.

This is the v2.0 operational foundation. Managers should verify and enter the exact approved ratios and qualification wording from the current controlled SOP/RA documents before relying on compliance decisions.
