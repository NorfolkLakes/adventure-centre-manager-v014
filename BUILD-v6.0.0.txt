Adventure Centre Manager v6.0.0

Programme Builder changes:
- Auto Fill Programme button added above the programme grid.
- Programme Test runs after Auto Fill and Update Programme.
- Groups in the same school are checked for matching activity sets.
- Standard activities are checked for unintended duplicates.
- Super Break and Outdoor Challenge Canoe/Kayak use two-session blocks.
- Bargain Special Canoe/Kayak use one consecutive paired block.
- Backup activities remain available and are used only when requested activities cannot be placed.
